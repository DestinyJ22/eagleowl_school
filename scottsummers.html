Scott Summers
My name is Scott Summers but, my talent name is Cyclops. I am a member of a subspecies known as mutants, a person born with superhuman abilities, like most of the children here at our academy. I am very experienced when it comes to dealing with kids and people with superhuman abilities our as we like to call them... Talents and Gifts.
Information
I am a teacher here at the academy. I am in charge of the main superhuman abilities within the children and monitoring the children when they take the placement test to get a glimpse of what I am getting myself into.
Courses
I teach a few courses that allow talent management and discovering the students gifts and talents. These courses help the children understand when, where and how to use your talents and gifts. They also help the students find themselves within that gift they have and realy lets them get a feel for their personalities.
Extra
My classroom rules are simple: Do Not Use Your Powers Out Of Term! This means unless asked or the assignment requires you to, please do not use your powers because they can be very dangerous to the not only the people around you but also yourself if you do not know what you are doing.
